# Gemma CogniVault Functionalities

Based on the application's structure and documentation, here are the main functionalities of Gemma CogniVault from a user's point of view:

### 1. 📚 Private Document Library (Knowledge Sync)
* **Local PDF Uploads:** You can drag and drop or select your own PDF files to build a personal knowledge base.
* **Live Progress Tracking:** When you upload large documents, the app provides a dynamic UI to show you exactly how far along the processing engine is.
* **100% Data Privacy:** Your files are processed entirely on your machine. Nothing is ever sent to the cloud, ensuring total privacy for sensitive documents.

### 2. 🤖 Intelligent Chatbot (Knowledge Base)
* **Chat with Your Data:** You can ask questions in a conversational chat interface, and the AI will read through your uploaded PDFs to give you direct answers.
* **Sub-millisecond Retrieval:** Because the app keeps your document indexes in your computer's RAM, searching for information feels instantaneous.

### 3. 🛡️ Crash-Proof Processing
* **Pause & Resume:** If you are uploading a massive library of documents and your computer dies, crashes, or you need to close the app, you won't lose your progress. When you restart the app, it will pick up processing from the exact page it left off on without you having to re-upload anything.

### 4. ⚙️ App Settings
* **Configuration:** There is a dedicated Settings view where you can manage your preferences or view the status of your local environment (like the Ollama models).
